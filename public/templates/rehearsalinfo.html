Things to know for your dance rehearsal


Dressing for lessons is fairly easy! Here are a few key pointers:
  • Wear clothing you can comfortably move in.
  • Think of what you might wear to a yoga class or to go running!
  • Have layers! Make sure you can remove layers as you warm up through the lesson.
  • Bring a pair of tennis shoes which have clean soles! ( studios do not allow outdoor footwear).
  • Have a pair of shoes that are similar or the same pair you will wear for the dance at the wedding.
  • Women: If you will be wearing a dress, bring a skirt that is similar to your dress for the wedding.
  • Bring some extra water
